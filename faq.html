FAQ's


Can I Edit My Order ?

Your order can be edited before it reaches your Table.
Once order is placed and restaurant starts preparing your food, you may not edit its contents


I want to Cancel My Order

We will do our best to accommodate your request if the order is not placed.
Please note that we will have a right to charge a cancellation fee up to full order value to compensate our restaurant if your order has been confirmed.


Will you be accountable for quality/quantity?

Quantity and quality of the food is the restaurants' responsibility. However in case of
issues with the quality or quantity, kindly submit your feedback and we will try to improve it.


Is there a minimum order value?

We have no minimum order value and you can order for any amount


Do you support bulk orders ?

In order to provide all customers with a great selection and to ensure on time delivery of
your meal, we reserve the right to limit the quantities depending on supply


Can I order in advance?

We currently do not support this functionality. All our orders are placed and executed
on-demand
